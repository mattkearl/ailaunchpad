HOW IT WORKS

3. Describe

4. Create

PRO TIPS

Focus on a single feature

Start with one core feature that solves a specific problem. You can always expand your project later.

Be specific with your answers

The more details you provide to bolt.new, the better your results will be. Think about colors, layouts, and functionality.

Describe the user experience

Explain how people will interact with your creation. What will they see first? How will they navigate?

Build in iterations

Create a simple version first, then enhance it step by step. This approach leads to better results.
